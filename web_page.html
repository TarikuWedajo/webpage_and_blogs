What Mercato handles

Point of Sale & Inventory

Quick sales, stock tracking, low-stock alerts, customers and suppliers, printable VAT receipts with TIN.

Double-Entry Accounting

Automatic chart of accounts, general ledger, and trial balance aligned to IFRS for SMEs. One sale, fully accounted for.

VAT

15% handled automatically, with monthly VAT returns generated straight from your books and ready to export.

Payroll

PAYE on the current 2025 brackets, pension (7% / 11%), payslips, and one-click salary and tax remittance.

Withholding Tax

Applied automatically when you pay suppliers, with certificates recorded.

Corporate Income Tax

Year-end CIT, Minimum Alternative Tax, and quarterly advance tax, computed for you.

Fixed Assets

Asset register with both IFRS and Ethiopian tax depreciation.

Imports

Landed-cost capitalisation, recoverable import VAT, and the 3% advance income tax, all in one entry.
